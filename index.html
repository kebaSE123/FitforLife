About Fit4Life

The BIHL Trust in collaboration with Bomaid is bringing back the Fit for Life campaign, a holistic health and fitness programme geared towards promoting a healthy lifestyle and wellness for Batswana. We are pursuing the following objectives:

  • Encouraging healthy living through eating well, incorporating fresh fruits and vegetables, water intake, getting enough rest, and other useful methods of staying healthy
  • Promoting healthy living through increasing activity levels, avoiding a non-active lifestyle
  • Discouraging exposure to risk factors i.e. High alcohol consumption, smoking, unhealthy diets
  • Encouraging regular health screenings offered to members by Bomaid

This year we are bringing together incredible 400 contestants from Gaborone and Francistown in our quest for a healthier nation!

REQUIREMENTS

Who can apply?
Anyone aged between 21-60 years
To qualify you must:

  • Currently live a non-active lifestyle
  • Weigh above 84kg (Men)
  • Weigh above 75kg (Women)
